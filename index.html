About the Campaign

Green Earth is a global tree-planting initiative committed to restoring nature and protecting the environment. Since our inception, we have planted hundreds of thousands of trees, creating green spaces, supporting wildlife habitats, and reducing carbon footprints. By joining our campaign, you contribute directly to a healthier planet, helping combat climate change while fostering sustainable communities for future generations.

  • Restoration of natural habitats
  • Improvement of air quality
  • Support for local communities
